Conclusion Section

From this study, I learned that most of my friends did not like candles. The participants were extremely familiar with computers and the internet. Many participants regularly puchased items online. From a sample size of four, it is hard to generalize using statistics. However, based on the reports on usability, both websites were easy to use. Participants were already quite familiar with purchasing items online. The usability studie focused on website design. The participants were trouble-shooting common problems that users may have in finding the right item.
