Project: Part III - Usability Evaluation

Jung Mee Park is a M.A. in Library and Information Science student at the University of Arizona. Her research interests include social science librarianship and data use in the social sciences. Dr. Park holds a BA in history and sociology from the University of Pennsylvania, MA in Quantitative Methods in Social Sciences from Columbia University, and PhD in Sociology from Cornell University. Her publications have appeared in the American Journal of Sociology and International Area Studies Review. She is a visiting scholar at the University of Southern California (USC), where she previously worked as a postdoctoral fellow.
