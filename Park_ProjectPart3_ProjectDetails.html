The study is based on 4 participants answering 3 surveys. Each participant answered one survey on their demographics information, the second survey inquired about their user experience on the Yankee Candle website, and the third survey asked about Goose Creek Candles website. The candle website questionnaires used Likert scales. The demographics survey included questions on gender, age, and computer usage. The four surveys were conducted virtually. The researcher emailed the copies of the instructions, the demographics survey, and two usability surveys.

Participants

My participants included four adults with graduate degrees (3 held PhDs, 1 completed a Master's). Even though all four participants frequently bought items online, none purchased candles online.
